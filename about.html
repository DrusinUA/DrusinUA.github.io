About Me

I am Dimitris, a Game Director, Motion and Game Designer, Writer, and Storyteller with 4 years of experience. My journey in game development has been driven by a passion for creating immersive and memorable gaming experiences. From motion design to narrative writing, I have honed my skills to deliver high-quality games that resonate with players.

My most recent project, Thief’s Shelter, has been a culmination of my skills and vision as a game director. It represents my commitment to storytelling and creating rich, atmospheric worlds that captivate the player.
