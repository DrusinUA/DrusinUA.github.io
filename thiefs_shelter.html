Game Overview

Thief’s Shelter is set in the year 1965, in a world where corruption runs deep within the English police force, and street gangs rule the night. At the heart of the story is Detective James, who, despite suffering from artificial dementia and being haunted by a demon, is tasked with solving a mysterious homicide in Birmingham. As you guide James through this investigation, you must navigate his delusions and uncover the truth hidden within the sinister Red Order.

Key Features:

  • Dynamic narrative that changes based on your choices.
  • Immersive 3D environments with a point-and-click adventure style.
  • Optional events and VHS tapes that reveal hidden lore.
  • Complex puzzles and riddles that test your investigative skills.

The Development Journey

The development of Thief’s Shelter has been a labor of love and a journey of perseverance. Over the past four years, our small team has faced numerous challenges, from technical hurdles to creative roadblocks. As the lead developer, I’ve been deeply involved in every aspect of the game, from writing and directing to motion and game design. Together with George Rontoulis, we’ve crafted a game that reflects our vision and passion for storytelling.

Challenges:

  • Managing a two-person core team while outsourcing specialized tasks like voice overs and music composition.
  • Balancing the game’s pacing and atmosphere to create an immersive experience.
  • Overcoming technical limitations and ensuring the game runs smoothly across multiple platforms.

Current State: The game is currently in the final stages of development, with polishing and final testing underway. We are committed to delivering a high-quality experience that will resonate with players around the world.
