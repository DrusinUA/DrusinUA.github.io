Game Overview

Thief’s Shelter is an immersive adventure set in 1965, where players dive into a world rife with corruption and dark secrets. As Detective James, you are tasked with solving a mysterious homicide in Birmingham, all while grappling with artificial dementia and being haunted by a demon. Every choice you make carries weight, influencing the outcome of the investigation and the fate of the characters you encounter.

Inspired by classic point-and-click adventure games, Thief’s Shelter brings that thoughtful structure into a fully interactive 3D space, offering both familiar and innovative experiences for players. The game’s environments are meticulously crafted, ensuring that every corner holds a secret waiting to be discovered.

Dimitris’ vision for Thief’s Shelter was to create a game where narrative and gameplay coexist harmoniously. His personal connection to the project is evident in every aspect, from the atmospheric design to the intricate storytelling. The environment itself becomes a character, shaping the narrative and driving the player’s decisions.

Thief’s Shelter features a dynamic narrative that adapts to your choices, encouraging deep exploration and rewarding curiosity. The puzzles are designed to be challenging yet intuitive, offering a satisfying experience for players who enjoy a thoughtful, deliberate pace.
