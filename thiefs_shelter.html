Game Overview

Thief’s Shelter is set in the year 1965, in a world where corruption runs deep within the English police force, and street gangs rule the night. At the heart of the story is Detective James, who, despite suffering from artificial dementia and being haunted by a demon, is tasked with solving a mysterious homicide in Birmingham. As you guide James through this investigation, you must navigate his delusions and uncover the truth hidden within the sinister Red Order.

Key features include a dynamic narrative that changes based on your choices, immersive 3D environments with a point-and-click adventure style, and complex puzzles that test your investigative skills.
