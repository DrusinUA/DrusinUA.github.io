Privacy Policy

Restless Corp respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to our website (restlesscorp.com) and to our games published by Restless Corp.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not create user accounts on our website or in our games, and we do not collect account credentials such as usernames or passwords.

Information We Collect

Our games integrate Unity Analytics (the default, out‑of‑the‑box analytics service provided by Unity Technologies) to help us understand gameplay and improve quality. Unity Analytics provides aggregated, non‑identifying usage data such as event counts (e.g., level starts/completions), device and platform type, app version, and general technical diagnostics. We do not use Unity Analytics to collect personally identifying information, and we do not attempt to identify you from this data.

For details about Unity’s data practices, please review Unity’s Privacy Policy: unity.com/legal/privacy-policy.

How We Use Information

We use analytics to monitor stability, diagnose crashes, balance gameplay, and guide product decisions. We do not sell your data. We share data only with service providers that process it on our behalf (for example, Unity) and only for the purposes described above, or if required by law.

Data Retention

We retain analytics data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy and in accordance with our service providers’ standard retention periods.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

Your Rights

Depending on your location (for example, the EEA/UK under the GDPR or California under the CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your information. To exercise these rights or ask questions, please contact us via the Contacts section on our website.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Links to Other Sites

Our website and games may link to third‑party sites or services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies; we are not responsible for their content or practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date below.
