Description

Repentant is a dramatic point & click adventure game with hand-painted art.
Repentant is partially a sequel to the game Little Kite, but it can be played by a person who is not familiar with Little Kite.

Story

A seasoned criminal, Oliver is consumed by remorse for the harm his actions brought to his family. Failed attempts to reconnect with his ex-wife deepen his spiral into alcohol dependence. One day, in a small shop, he witnesses a robbery committed by a young woman.
In that moment, he sees a chance to do one thing right — by helping someone else.
But who truly needs help — and who is the victim? It remains to be seen.

Features

Explore atmospheric hand-painted locations and solve challenging puzzles
Experience an intriguing story with an unexpected ending
Original soundtrack by Abstracode and full English voice acting
