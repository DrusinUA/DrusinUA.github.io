Description

This is a deep social point&click adventure drama about tough childhood and self-sacrifice.
The Safe Place is part of a trilogy from the developer of Little Kite and Repentant.

Story

Alexander accidentally kills a man. A witness, a young woman, asks him to help solve her own problems in exchange for silence. What begins as a compromise quickly spirals out of control.

Features

Fully voiced dialogue in English
Your dialogue choices influence the story
Beautiful hand-drawn visuals
