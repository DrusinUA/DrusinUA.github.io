Description

Little Kite is a story-driven point-and-click adventure about Mary and her son Andrew as they try to rebuild their lives after loss.
It explores the struggles of a family under pressure, where one person gives up and another fights to break the cycle.

Story

The main characters are Mary and her son Andrew. After the death of her husband, Mary married for a second time, to Oliver, in an attempt to create a full-fledged family for her son.
Over time, Oliver seeks solace in alcohol and their home slips into domestic violence.
Mary must confront a difficult truth and choose a path that protects her son.

Features

Dramatic story about Mary and Andrew
Two playable main characters
A lot of puzzles with different difficulties
Two locations: the real world and Andrew's fantasy world. Each of them has its own unique atmosphere.
