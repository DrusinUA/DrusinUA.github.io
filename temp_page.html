Description

You are about to be a part of an adventure of the Dark Age. Follow Rick in his damned path of vengeance and help him to redeem his soul.The life of your beloved is in danger. Hurry up and make a wise choice. Along the way complete challenging platforming levels and upgrade your powers.

Story

The story unfolds in the Middle Ages, a time of plague, witch hunts and towering church authority. You play as Rick, a hunter drawn into a fight for redemption.
Rick''s wife, Rose, is accused of witchcraft and seized for execution. In trying to defend her, Rick is thrown into a dungeon. He must escape and find a way to save her.
In the depths he meets a witch named Amelia, who spares his life and offers aid. Clues scattered through the labyrinth reveal the truth behind the darkness.
Crucial dialogue choices shape the ending, inviting you to investigate the mystery at the heart of Rick''s fate.
